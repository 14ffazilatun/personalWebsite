Extracirriculars

My responsibilities included teaching students in both SHSAT and SAT topics, preparing and organizing worksheets and test packets to ensure they reached the correct classes on time, and maintaining communication with parents about their child’s progress and grades. This role allowed me to develop strong organizational skills and gain experience in both instruction and parent communication.

For two years, I’ve been an active member of my school’s environmental club, where I managed the school’s compost bin, educating peers on proper waste disposal to support our school garden. Additionally, I led a team in assembling aquaponic growing towers and helped underclassmen understand and maintain optimal water pH levels for plant growth.
